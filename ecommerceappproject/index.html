Aeroponic Tower

Aeroponic is a inovative way of growing sustainable food with higher yield and less spacing.Commercial aeroponic systems can be costly. The average home gardener, however, can design an aeroponic growing system!

Things you will need to build a aeroponic tower DIY will include:

Large Storage Bins


PVC pipes


PVC end caps


Spray taps


T-fitting


Hose


Adapter


Netted pots
